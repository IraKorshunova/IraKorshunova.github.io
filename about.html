I am a master student in Statistical Data Analysis at Ghent University in Belgium. At the same time I'm finishing my master studies in Applied Mathematics at National Technical University of Ukraine, where I received a Bachelor degree in 2012.

For nearly two years I've been doing a research at Reservoir Lab. Mainly, I've been exploring an application of convolutional neural networks to EEG data for epileptic seizures detection and prediction. I obtained pretty good results, which will be published in a follow-up AES Seizure Prediction Challenge paper. I'm planning to finalize this research by analyzing what convnets can learn from a raw EEG signal (previously I've applied them to sort of spectrograms).

In my free time I like playing cello. Currently, I am cellist at Ghent University Symphony Orchestra.
